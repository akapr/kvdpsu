Pittsburgh Restaurant Microreviews

Pete Su
Roger Rosner

This page lists restaurants that we know and either love or hate in Pittsburgh. The opinions here are our own, and try to be as honest and brief as possible. The listing is alphabetical. Maybe later when it is larger, we'll list by category as well.

Casbah - In Shadyside on Highland Ave.

Reasonably good mediterranean-ish food. Too often more expensive than it is worth.

Chaya - Murray Ave, halfway down the hill.

Good Sushi and other Japanese food. Nice hot pots. Get the live scallop if they have it.

La Cucina Flegrea - Murray Ave, halfway down the hill.

Best Italian in Pittsburgh. Go on Wednsdays for Risotto Night.

Tasty - In Shadyside on Highland Ave in the plaza just before the bridge.

Some excellent home style Cantonese. Order off of the "Hong Kong" menu only.

Vivo - Main St. in Bellevue.

Very expensive. Very excellent. No credit cards.
